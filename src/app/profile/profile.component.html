President / co-founder

Paul Courtney


Mr. Courtney has led successful Federal contracting efforts ranging in size from $200k to over $250 million. He has successfully run 26 Prime Federal Contracts, 13 at the Defense Intelligence Agency, and has never lost a re-compete of a prime contract.

Mr. Courtney began The Buffalo Group with his business partner Paul Schlee in order to provide the highest level of service to the federal government.
